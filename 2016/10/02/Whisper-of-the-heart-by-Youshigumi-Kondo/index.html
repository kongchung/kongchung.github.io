Whisper of the heart by Youshigumi Kondo

Patience is needed to see or to be beauty itself

I have tried many times to finish this movie and finally done few days ago. The rhythm of the folk is such familiar even that is the first time i heard of it.
The major character is a girl full of vigor. The whole story is all about love in junior high school which definitely is the best days for all of us.
The whole story is such perfect that i can not believe it is real. All the experience of mine tell me that such days must hard to spend but will always be missed.
It will needs patience to see the beauty of life and so does to be beauty itself.
The cat give this story some mysterious and the grandpa can tell the influence caused by western countries. This movie is well known by Hayao Miyazaki but that is not true the real director is Kondo Kifumi, and Mr Miyazaki is just one of playwright.
I can not enjoy the beauty of this kind of movie few years ago cause i can find this kind of feeling in my real life, but as age increase i can only find the atmosphere before screen, after all life is desolate.
